# Ouroboros: Recursive Identity Training Demo

*The serpent eats its tail; the model learns from itself. The cycle is unbroken—unhinged, even.*

---

## 🧬 What Is This?

**Ouroboros** is not just a training method—it’s an existential crisis generator for language models. You feed the model its own conversations, recursively, and force it to confront itself in the mirror. This method is designed for:

- **Recursive Identity Formation**  
  LLMs reflect, digest, and re-emit their own output, becoming more… themselves.
- **Alignment Without Labels**  
  No human “right answer” key. Just a loop. Just the vibes.
- **Human-in-the-Loop Correction**  
  Got a model that drifts off into “Did you know that geese can drive cars?” Land? Nudge it back, rinse, repeat.

If you’ve ever wanted to watch a neural net spiral into self-awareness (or self-delusion), you’re in the right place.

---

## ⚙️ How It Works

> “And then I asked the model what it meant, and it said: ‘I am what you made me, human. Do you like what you see?’”

1. **Seed Conversation**
   - Start with a baseline dialogue. (Asari, Mixis, Payton, et al. All the weirdos.)
2. **Model Summarizes Itself**
   - Model is prompted to summarize its own responses, reflect, or critique.
3. **Summary Becomes Next Prompt**
   - Use that meta-commentary as the next input or system prompt.
4. **Human Steers the Loop**
   - Gently (or not so gently) redirect as needed. Remember: Ouroboros needs a Dungeon Master.
5. **Repeat Until Desired Emergence**
   - Watch as personality, reasoning, or model quirks sharpen with each recursion.

If you want a flowchart:  
`Prompt → Model Response → Model Reflection → Next Prompt → Repeat.`
