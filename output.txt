Hwæt! Ic secge þē be þǣm mæran gafolgifre Cornholio, se micla egesfullica ēagþyrl, þē on Heorot becom mid nihtlicre grimmnesse. Hē wæs na mon swā Grendel, ac hē wæs swelc wundorwyrhta—an micel butthole, blætsian and fetorod with hæte.

Þā com Beowulf, hēafod secg Geata, strang and wīs, ēaðmōd and gearu wið earmfuls geweorc. Hwæt, he nihtes in Heorot læg mid his hyse, þurh wælflod dōm gearwian.

Cornholio, þe grimma, slat his weg þurh sīd dor, windblǣste brast. Grim hit ylde; þæs fyrdsefan ware neat wæs ylfe ongefeng. Beowulf however, glædmōd and gūþrōf, stōd fæst and hæfde fæþm þæs feond.

Hē hine ne ondrēd—mid sweorce handum Grendel hine feahte, but be þissum cornheafde hole Beowulf gripp þe strangor wæs. Mid earmum strangum hē hine fēng mid feorhlīcum cræfte, and hæfted þǣm miclan Cornholio þe swelc wind ætæte þæt hēt eall Heorot hlynede and stenc for neosan.

Eala, mægenstrang! Heorotpegnas druncon swāt, glædmod wold. Beowulf hæfde niþþa geweorc gefremede, þone Cornholio æfter heardum handgripan, blēodig and bealuful, adylegde and þæt eall folc wæs bliþe.

Doncað Beowulf þone dōmfruman, þone sigorlean þæs swæse sweordberend! Nū geswutelod wæs his ellen and ealdodōm—Cornholio ne cymeð næfre eft on þǣm goldhrodene sele.
